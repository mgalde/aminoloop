Non-synthetic. Bioactive. American-made.

AminoLoop is a hydrolyzed protein biostimulant derived from animal sources, processed to be fully sterile, and produced entirely in the United States. It delivers concentrated amino acids, peptides, and mineral nutrients, the building blocks plants use for growth, stress response, and yield.

Unlike synthetic fertilizers, AminoLoop works with soil biology. Applied to the root zone, it feeds the microbial communities that make nutrients available to plants, activating the soil, not just spiking it.
